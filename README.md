⭐ Olist E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard
📊 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI Business Intelligence dashboard built using the Olist E-Commerce dataset.
It analyzes sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and geographical distribution to support data-driven business decision-making.

🛠 Tools & Technologies
Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data Modeling
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Data Visualization

📌 Key Business Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance
Identify top-performing products and categories
Understand customer purchasing behavior
Evaluate regional sales distribution
Track payment method preferences

📊 Dashboard Features
🔹 Executive Overview
Total Revenue
Total Orders
Total Customers
Average Order Value
Revenue Trend Analysis
Payment Method Breakdown

👥 Customer Analytics
Customer distribution analysis
Revenue per customer
Order frequency patterns
Repeat vs new customers
🛍 Product Performance
Top-selling product categories
Revenue contribution by category
Quantity sold analysis
Product demand trends

🌍 Geographical Analysis
Revenue by state
Revenue by city
Customer distribution by region
Order concentration analysis

💡 Key Business Insights
São Paulo contributed the highest revenue and customer base, making it the key market region.
Credit Card was the most preferred payment method, showing strong digital payment adoption.
Health & Beauty and Watches & Gifts were the top-performing product categories.
Customer activity is highly concentrated in major urban regions, indicating regional dependency in sales.

🎯 Business Impact

This dashboard helps businesses:
Improve sales strategy
Identify high-value customers
Optimize product categories
Understand regional market behavior
